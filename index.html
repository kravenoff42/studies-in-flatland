One of my favorite books of all time is the incredibly nerd, Adventures in Flatland. Which tells the story of a two-dimesional character's encounter with a three-dimensional being. The story also adresses the lower dimesions as well in a very charming way, there's a link in the credits section if you're interested. I highly recommend it.

Recently, I came across a tutorial video put together my the incredibly nerdy, Dan Shiffman, that reminded me of that story, all about 2D projections of 3D objects. So, in the spirit of Edwin A. Abbott, here are my studies in Flatland.

First thing to talk about are tables, in Mathematics circles these are called marices, but I hate spelling that word, and the format confuses me so if you are already familiar with the subject, I apologize in advance.

Lets start with the easiest example. A single point in 2D.
